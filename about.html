About the Series

Some reflections show us the past. Some reveal the present. And some show us what we're becoming.

What is Satoshi's Mirror?

Satoshi's Mirror is a 12-part anime documentary series that traces Bitcoin's evolution from its birth in the 2008 financial crisis to its impact on the modern world. Through mature animation inspired by Ghost in the Shell and Cowboy Bebop, we explore not just the technology, but the philosophy, economics, and human belief that shaped a revolution.

Every revolution begins with a question. Every system, with doubt. This series examines how a handful of believers created something that challenged the very foundations of money, trust, and power.

Visual Style

Drawing inspiration from mature anime classics, the series combines:

  • Ghost in the Shell - Philosophical depth and cyberpunk aesthetics
  • Cowboy Bebop - Jazz-noir atmosphere and character-driven storytelling
  • Golgo 13 - Serious tone and documentary realism
  • Berserk - Dark atmospheric lighting for crisis moments

Episode Structure

The series spans Bitcoin's history across 12 episodes, each exploring a pivotal moment, character, or concept that shaped the movement. From Satoshi's whitepaper to the pizza transaction, from Hal Finney's contributions to the community's growth, we document the real people and events that created this revolution.
